A heart for little smiles

Sprouting Smiles Pediatric Dentistry has become a name San Diego parents pass along to friends. The practice grew from one simple belief, that a child's comfort and dental health go hand in hand, and that idea shapes every visit.

Dr. Wendy Yang leads the team with a gentle, unhurried touch that quickly wins over both kids and parents. A Southern California native, she earned her MS in Oral Biology at UCLA, her DDS at the University of Illinois, Chicago, and completed her pediatric residency at UC San Francisco.

Beyond her training, families love how warm and patient she is, especially with nervous children. Outside the office she enjoys cooking, dancing, traveling and languages.

Dr. Wendy is a board certified pediatric dentist, recognized by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry and the American Board of Pediatric Dentistry.
